Tesla Sales Overview Dashboard

This is a dynamic dashboard created with Microsoft Power Bi which provides a summary of insights drawn from their data

Project Overview

This is an interactive dashboard visualizing Tesla Sales Data over the years.
The dashboard is easy to use and interpret the data as it is designed with simple and appropriate visuals.
Visualization tool: POWER BI
Database: Excel datasets from Kaggle
Objective of the dashboard:
1. To provide the details of the Total Sales made by the Company
2. To provide profit margins made from the company sales
3. To provide details on the companys profit margins based on the total units sold.
The process involved:
1. Data is extracted from Kaggle, transformed and loaded with power query into Power BI.
2. Identification of different measures and creating useful measures with using DAX.
3. Validating the data and the created measures.
4. Creating the dashboard utilizing the appropriate visuals.

Key Metrics and the diverse charts showing the sales, profits and the units sold over the years are enumerated below:
1. Gross profits, Total Sales, Total Sold Units, and the Purchase type.
2. A donut chart to show the total sales, gross profits, and the total units sold by the car model.
3. Clustered column chart to display the gross profits, and the total units sold based on model version.
4. A donut chart to show gross profits, and the total units by purchase type.
5. A column chart to visualize the gross profits, and the total units by country sold.
6. A line graph showing the gross profits, and the total units distribution in a year.
Selecting data:
The dashboard has two slicers, a tiled slicer to select the year(s) to display and another one to select the months to display the data.
Navigation:
The dashboard has three buttons to navigate the Sales, Profits and the Stock (total units sold) pages displaying their respective details.
